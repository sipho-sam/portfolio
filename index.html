Mood
U.S. Cannabis Lifestyle Brand  ·  mood.com
Active engagement

Mood is a hemp-derived cannabinoid brand competing in one of the most compliance-sensitive and competitive niches in the U.S. market. I was brought on to architect and lead the brand's GEO/AEO content strategy from the ground up.

The work spans full-funnel content production, AI Overview optimisation, LLM citation strategy, and technical SEO — designed to make Mood the authoritative answer across both traditional search and generative AI platforms including ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews.

In 8 months, organic blog traffic grew by +198%. The brand now holds 7,413+ LLM and AI Overview citations — establishing Mood as a cited authority at the forefront of AI-powered search.
